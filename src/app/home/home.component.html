Frequently Asked Questions

How does TESTINY work?

Create an account as a developer or recruiter. Developers can take tests, while recruiters can upload and manage technical assessments.

How do I take a test?

Once a recruiter uploads a test, it will appear in your dashboard. You can then start the test at your convenience.

How does the anti-cheat system work?

Your camera and microphone must be enabled, you should be alone, and all security conditions must be met to access the test.

What are the best practices for taking a test?

Ensure a quiet environment, avoid switching tabs, and have a clear background behind you.

How do I get my test results?

Once you complete the test, you will receive your score immediately on your dashboard.
